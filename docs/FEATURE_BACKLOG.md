# Feature Backlog

This backlog records product direction, not ordered commitments.

## Authentication and Security

- Password reset and email verification
- Protected routes and role routing
- Admin and teacher role management
- Security Rules and Emulator tests

## Families and Dancers

- Parent and family editing
- Additional parent accounts and emergency contacts
- Dancer editing, archive, photos, and team assignments
- Enrollment, attendance, and progress history

## Classes and Scheduling

- Class catalog and weekly schedules
- Teachers, rooms, capacity, and waitlists
- Enrollments, trials, rehearsals, and calendar export

## Teachers and Attendance

- Teacher profiles and rosters
- Attendance, lesson plans, music, and choreography notes
- Absence reasons, makeup classes, and reports

## Communication

- Studio and team announcements
- Email and in-app notifications
- Schedule, tuition, costume, and competition reminders

## Billing

- Tuition, registration fees, invoices, and discounts
- Payment plans, Stripe Checkout, receipts, and refunds
- Failed payments and reports

Stripe is planned but not implemented.

## Waivers

- Liability, media, and medical authorization
- Versioned templates, signatures, reminders, and audit history

## Competition Hub

- Teams, competitions, conventions, and Nationals
- Fees, costumes, packing lists, handbooks, fundraising, and travel

## Private Lessons

- Availability, requests, approval, booking, billing, and cancellations

## Administration and Platform

- Family management
- Student search and pagination
- Staff management and studio settings
- Reports, exports, and audit history
- Tests, monitoring, analytics, backups, and deployment
